Guidelines & Policies

Understanding library rules helps ensure fair access and a respectful environment for everyone.

Library Policies

Rules & Procedures

Adhering to library rules and regulations ensures a productive environment. Noncompliance may be grounds for suspension of library privileges or expulsion from the university.

General Rules

  • The PUP ID is required and should be pinned while inside the University premises.
  • Library Account Number is required when borrowing materials.
  • Appointment is required for all those who want to visit the library.
  • An accomplished Health Declaration Form is required upon entry in the building.
  • Proper attire is to be observed. Attire must conform to standards of decency.
  • All users must silence or turn off their cell phones while in the library. No cell phone conversations are permitted.
  • The use of laptops is allowed, although the library provides a limited number of computers for public use.
  • Mutilation and defacing of a book or any other library resources, vandalism, and damaging of any library property shall be punishable by suspension or expulsion.
  • Loud conversations, laughter, smoking, playing games, littering, and display of public affection are strictly prohibited.

Borrowing & Returning — Fiction Books

Fiction books may be borrowed and taken out for a week. The books may be renewed for another week.

  • All library materials borrowed under the name of a particular borrower shall be the registered borrower's responsibility.
  • The borrower shall clear himself/herself of his/her accountabilities at the end of each semester or summer session.

Borrowing & Returning — General References

Reference materials, serials, special collections, theses, dissertations, and nonprint resources are for Room-use only.

  • Graduate School and College of Law resources may be borrowed for two (2) days depending upon the availability of copies.

Guidelines on the Use of E-Library

The PUP-CLFI e-Learning Center provides free use of computers for online searching. The following rules are to be followed:

  • Each bona fide user is required to register at the counter and should present their ID and/or current Registration Card with picture upon entering the facility.
  • Users are not allowed to view pornography and play games with the computer.
  • Users are required to observe silence and courtesy during their stay in the area.
  • Eating, drinking, laughing, sleeping and other disruptive behavior are prohibited inside the area.
  • Proper dress code is required.
  • Only one person or user is allowed per computer in the internet workstation.
  • Borrowers will be held responsible for any damage on the equipment issued to them.

Library Privileges & Membership

The following may avail of the library services:

  • All bona fide students of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines.
  • All administrators, faculty and employees of PUP.
  • Alumni of the Polytechnic University of the Philippines.
  • Researchers from other institutions.
  • Friends of the PUP Libraries.

Library Clearance

The following are required to secure a clearance from the University Library:

  • University officials, faculty and administrative personnel applying for sick/vacation/sabbatical leaves, resignation, retirement.
  • Students applying for graduation, and honorable dismissal, as well as returning students and the like.
